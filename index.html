What is GHST

The next generation of the Ghana Cedi.

The Ghana Cedis Token (GHST) is a fiat-backed stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the Ghana Cedi. Every token in circulation is backed by an equivalent amount of Ghana Cedis held in a segregated custodial account at a Bank of Ghana-licensed commercial bank.

GHST brings the reliability of the Cedi together with the speed, transparency, and programmability of blockchain technology — enabling instant, near-zero-cost payments for Ghanaians everywhere.

1. Introduction

The Ghana Cedis Token (GHST) is a fiat-backed digital asset representing the Ghana Cedi on blockchain infrastructure. Issued by Cede Stable LTD, GHST is designed to harness the efficiency, transparency, and programmability of blockchain-based payments while maintaining full monetary sovereignty, regulatory oversight, and the stability of the Ghana Cedi.

GHST operates on the same fundamental economic principles as existing licensed electronic money products in Ghana. The only substantive difference is the underlying technology infrastructure — blockchain rather than proprietary telecom systems.

5. Compliance Framework

GHST is designed to operate within the full regulatory framework established by the Bank of Ghana. This includes alignment with the Virtual Asset Service Providers (VASP) Act 2025, the Anti-Money Laundering Act 2020, and the Data Protection Act 2012.

A multi-layered KYC/AML compliance framework implements tiered verification levels — from basic identification through enhanced due diligence — exceeding standards currently applied to mobile money operators.

6. Mint & Burn Lifecycle

The mint-and-burn mechanism ensures the total supply of GHST always matches the reserves held in custody:

StepActionDescription
1Deposit (Mint)User deposits GHS into the designated custodial account
2VerificationBanking partner confirms receipt via API webhook
3Token IssuanceEquivalent GHST minted on-chain and credited to wallet
4Transfer/UseUser transfers, pays merchants, or holds GHST
5Redemption (Burn)GHST burned; GHS disbursed to bank or mobile money

7. Supported Networks

GHST is currently live on the Base network (Ethereum L2) with plans to expand to the Stellar network. Multi-chain support enables broader accessibility and interoperability with the global stablecoin ecosystem, including USDC, EURC, and 475,000+ access points worldwide.

8. Risk Management

The GHST risk framework addresses liquidity risk (20% minimum cash buffer), technology risk (Stellar's 99.99% uptime, Fireblocks MPC), regulatory risk (proactive BoG engagement), cybersecurity risk (defense-in-depth architecture), and credit risk (sovereign-backed investments).
