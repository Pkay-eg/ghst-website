Token Whitepaper

A comprehensive overview of the Ghana Cedis Token — its design, economics, technology infrastructure, and regulatory framework. Issued by Cede Stable LTD.

1. Introduction

The Ghana Cedis Token (GHST) is a fiat-backed digital asset representing the Ghana Cedi on blockchain infrastructure. Issued by Cede Stable LTD, GHST is designed to harness the efficiency, transparency, and programmability of blockchain-based payments while maintaining full monetary sovereignty.

GHST operates on the same fundamental economic principles as existing licensed electronic money products in Ghana. The only substantive difference is the underlying technology infrastructure — blockchain rather than proprietary telecom systems.
